S. M. Chaudhari — Profile

Mr. Chaudhari, M.Tech from IIT Kharagpur, has been involved in consultancy and founded ECS. He carries 40+ years of knowledge in dairy, engineering, and projects, contributing to over 200 dairy projects.

He has worked with AMUL Unions, KMF Unions, Parag Food Pvt. Ltd (Gowardhan), VERKA Unions, Hatsun, Paras, Bhole Baba, Heinz, McKenzie, and more. His career began with NDDB (16 years), then Dynamic Dairy Industries (Chief Manager–Engineering for 2 years), followed by L&T Food Division (General Manager for 6 years) covering dairies, breweries, fruits & vegetables, and allied industries.

He assisted GCMMF in solving engineering problems for member dairies across Gujarat and supported future project planning. A unique achievement has been establishing a fully automated, state-of-the-art 100 MTPD powder plant—first of its kind in Asia—AMUL whey processing & powder plant, and the AMUL Vasai project.

His association spanned inception, estimating & costing, project reports, design & specifications, vendor development, supervision, execution, and commissioning. He has extensively visited dairy-rich countries (USA, France, Finland, Germany, Denmark, Australia, New Zealand) to deepen expertise.
